Common Questions

Frequently Asked Questions

What documents do I need for cross-border travel? ▾
Indian citizens need a valid government-issued photo ID (Aadhaar, Passport, or Voter ID). Nepali nationals need a valid citizenship certificate or passport. Children below 12 travelling with parents need a birth certificate or school ID. Always carry original documents — photocopies are not accepted at borders.
What is the cancellation and refund policy? ▾
Cancellations made 48+ hours before departure: 90% refund. 24–48 hours before: 70% refund. Less than 24 hours: 50% refund. No-shows receive no refund. Refunds are processed within 5–7 business days to the original payment method.
How much luggage am I allowed to carry? ▾
Each passenger is allowed up to 20 kg of check-in luggage and one small carry-on bag (under 7 kg). Extra luggage is charged at ₹50 per kg. Fragile or oversized items must be declared at booking. We do not accept livestock or hazardous materials.
Where are the boarding points in Bangalore? ▾
We have boarding points at BTM Layout (main office at 340 Jakkasandra Extension), Koramangala 1st Block, Electronic City Phase 1, and Whitefield. Our team will WhatsApp you the exact boarding point coordinates 24 hours before departure.
Is the bus GPS tracked? How can I share my location with family? ▾
Yes. All buses are GPS tracked. Once your journey begins, WhatsApp our support number for real-time location updates. A live tracking link will also be sent to your registered number 1 hour before departure, which you can share with family.
Do you offer child tickets or senior citizen discounts? ▾
Children below 5 years travel free if seated on a parent's lap. Children aged 5–12 receive a 30% discount. Senior citizens above 60 years receive a 10% discount on production of valid age proof at boarding. Discounts cannot be combined with coupon codes.
What payment methods do you accept? ▾
We accept UPI (Google Pay, PhonePe, Paytm), Debit/Credit Cards (Visa, Mastercard, RuPay), Net Banking, and Cash at our office. Online payments are processed securely via Razorpay. EMI options available on select cards for bookings above ₹5,000.
